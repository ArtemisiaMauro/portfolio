Projects

Soybean area prediction and impact on Brazilian forests report

Context : As part of a project carried out between December 2024 and January 2025 at IUT Paris Rive de Seine, I collaborated in a group of two to study the impact of increasing soybean acreage on deforestation in Brazil by 2030. We used two time series from FAOSTAT to predict these trends and understand the dynamics between agriculture and deforestation

Objectives :

  • Predict soybean acreage trends in Brazil up to 2030
  • Assess the impact of this development on Brazilian forest areas

Process and tools :

  • Data cleansing, structuring, analysis and trend visualization with RStudio
  • Report written in Word

Implemented skills :

Data extraction and processing, advanced statistical analysis, visualization, and drafting of usable conclusions
